# Online classroom

## One-liner

A web and mobile application for a medical university to create curricula and
conduct classes both online and offline.

## Context & problem

The university needed to streamline classroom activities: staff spent too much
effort preparing and running classes, and students had no digital way to track
their participation and progress.

## Process

Empathise → Define → Ideate → Prototype → Test → Design → Develop & Launch

- Conducted user interviews with staff & students, observed current classes to
  understand needs & pain points
- Refined problem statement & created user personas (staff & students)
- Brainstormed features, created low-fidelity prototypes for core functionalities
- Developed mid-fidelity prototypes for usability testing
- Conducted usability testing with staff & students, iterated on design
- Created high-fidelity prototypes & a style guide
- Design handoff, tested over developed web and mobile apps

## Solution highlights

- **Pre-Class Preparation** — prepare and share lecture materials, activities,
  and pre-class assessments to enhance student engagement
- **Interactive Classroom** — digital attendance via the mobile app, in-class
  quizzes/polls for participation and real-time feedback
- **Personalised Learning** — personalised assignments with evaluation and
  performance analysis to optimise learning outcomes
